# Incentives and Resource Stewardship

For decisions involving multiple actors, record who requested the work, who pays, who benefits, who bears downside risk, who controls the artifact, who may approve or stop, and which information boundaries affect the decision.

Do not infer hidden motives. Record declared goals, observable commitments, costs, benefits, risks, authority, conflicts, and unknowns.

Common failure modes include principal–agent mismatch, conflict of interest, hidden information, moral hazard, metric gaming, authority laundering, and shifting costs to parties without decision power.
