# Historical Vocabulary

Earlier Architectonic drafts used Greek-derived terms as compact conceptual labels. They are retained here only as historical context and compatibility references. Plain operational language is canonical.

```text
telos        purpose
ethos        conduct
nomos        authority and governance
oikonomia    incentives and resource stewardship
logos        reason-giving
episteme     justified knowledge
techne       reusable procedure
phronesis    situated judgment
physis       adaptation and continuity
```
